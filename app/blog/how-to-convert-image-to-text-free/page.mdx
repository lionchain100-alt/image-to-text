# How to Convert Image to Text for Free (2024)

Converting images to text used to require expensive software. Today, you can do it for free, directly in your browser — no installation, no sign-up.

## What You Need

Just a browser and an image. That's it.

## Step-by-Step

1. Go to [ImageToText](/)
2. Upload your image by dragging it in, clicking to browse, or pasting a screenshot with Ctrl+V
3. Select your language
4. Wait a few seconds for the OCR to finish
5. Copy or download the extracted text

## Tips for Better Accuracy

- Use high-resolution images
- Ensure good contrast between text and background
- Avoid blurry or skewed photos
- Crop to just the text area if possible

## Why Use a Browser-Based Tool?

Unlike desktop software, browser-based OCR tools require no installation and work on any device. Your files never leave your computer, so your data stays private.
